Those are the rules:

  • All writers must be 18 years old or older.
  • Only students can participate.
  • The script should be your own work and you should have all the rights in your possession.
  • The script should not be an adaptation of someone else's book or work. It should be your personal work.
  • All scripts should be in English. We do not mind international students participating but they can only submit scripts in English.
  • Members of the organization, judges and collaborators are not allowed to enter the contest.
  • Scripts must be the original work of the author and should not be encumbered by third person or entity. Adaptations of books are not allowed unless the book was written by you and you retained all the rights.
  • All entries should be paid either electronically or by money order. Personal checks are not accepted.
  • Entries payments should be received on the date or before the date of closing. In case they are late, the entry will automatically be transferred to the next period. The script will be screened in the next term.
  • If you wish to revise your draft, you must enter it as a new submission and pay the fee again.
  • The contest judges are not responsible for lost, stolen or damaged scripts during mailing or electronic transfer.
  • Scripts will not be returned so please keep a copy of your work.
  • Judges decisions are final and may not be disputed or challenged.
  • We accept scripts from all genres.
  • It does not matter to us if your name and address are on the script or not. Since we finance our own projects, we decide to work on it or not independable on the person. If your script is good, it does not matter to us who you are. If it is not, we will not put our money on it even if you were our close relative.
  • All submissions swhould be supported by a payment. We will not read your script if it is not paid for.
